Sugar, primarily sucrose, is used in a wide range of products across various industries. Hereâ€™s a breakdown of some of the common products made from sugar and their uses:

1. Granulated Sugar Uses: The most common form of sugar, granulated sugar is used in baking, cooking, sweetening beverages, and as a general sweetener in various food products.

2. Powdered (Confectioners') Sugar Uses: This finely ground sugar, often mixed with a small amount of cornstarch, is primarily used in frostings, icings, and as a dusting for pastries and desserts.

3. Brown Sugar Uses: Brown sugar contains molasses, giving it a distinct flavor. It is commonly used in baking, cooking, and sauces, especially in recipes for cookies, cakes, and barbecue sauces.

4. Molasses Uses: Molasses is a thick, dark syrup left after the sugar extraction process. It's used in baking (e.g., gingerbread), in making certain alcoholic beverages like rum, as a sweetener in sauces, and as a flavor enhancer in recipes like baked beans.
